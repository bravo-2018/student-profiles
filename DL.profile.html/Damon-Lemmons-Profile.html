About Me

Hi! I'm Damon. I've been described as patient, kind, calm, friendly, respectful and empathetic. I'm originally from Louisiana but after I left for college, I bounced around quite a bit. I have lived in...

  • Baton Rouge
  • Tucson (Arizona)
  • Phoenix
  • San Jose (CA)
  • San Francisco
  • San Diego

I'm new to San Diego and I'm excited because this city seems to blend everything that I loved about all the other places I've lived (beautiful weather, beachy vibe, friendly faces, progressive attitudes).

Professional

I've sort of gone overboard with this section of my life as I have held many jobs over the years. I have a BFA in Modern Dance and a Master's of Nonprofit Management. I also have a Clear CA K-8 Multiple Subjects Teaching Credential and an CA Administrative Credential. I have worked for a small professional dance company (briefly), an antibullying nonprofit agency, and an elementary school. My last six years were spent teaching 2nd grade and teaching a technology class to kids in kindergarten - 5th grade (computer teacher). I also served as an Interim Principal of an elementary school for about 7 months. That was a wild ride to say the least. Needless to say, I've decided to venture into the tech world and pursue web development at Learn Academy. I'm excited to bring along my life experiences, passions and interests in pursuit of a career that appeals to my affinity for puzzles, problem solving, introversion and technology.
