Posty Post

San Diego, CA, March 27, 2018

Hi! My name is Michael and I'm working towards becoming a software developer. Why? I've spent the last few months looking for jobs and interviewing in San Diego. I have a materials engineering degree and graduated at the top of my class from UCLA. My senior year, I started working for a lab in Malibu California, developing coatings for aerospace applications. Basically, I was a chemist half the day and material test engineer the other half. I resigned, hoping to find something that offered more growth potential and involved less hazardous chemicals. I moved in San Diego to live with my brother after taking a month-long trip across Europe. I thought I'd find a job in no time but it hasn't been easy as a materials engineer in a city where most of the engineering jobs are in biotech or computer science. Every day I would check for "engineering" jobs on indeed and see software engineering positions paying well. I had no idea what a "API", "MySQL", "React", "node.js" was. (to be continued..)
